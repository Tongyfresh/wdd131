Site Plan: Bonsai Basics

Site Name: Bonsai Basics

I wanted to do some personal research into the topic of beginner bonsai keeping and was hoping to make it a more approachable topic.

Optional domain: bonsaibeginnings.org

Site Purpose

My goal here is to create a friendly space for anyone curious about bonsai but not sure where to start. I'll break down the essentials—like picking a tree, potting it, and keeping it alive—to make the whole process feel less intimidating and give beginners the confidence to get their hands dirty.

Scenarios

  • "I just received a juniper bonsai as a gift. How do I take care of it so it doesn't die?"
  • "What are the best and most forgiving types of trees for a complete beginner to start with?"

Color Schema

I'm going for a natural, calming vibe with the colors, pulling inspiration straight from the garden.

  • Primary Green (#2A4B3A): This will be for my main headings and important accents. It has a nice, strong, earthy feel that reminds me of a healthy plant.
  • Accent Coral (#E07A5F): I'll use this for links and buttons. It's a warm, terracotta-like color that should stand out nicely without being jarring.
  • Dark Text (#3D405B): For all the main text. It's a dark, soft color that's easy on the eyes for reading.
  • Light Background (#F4F1DE): This will be the main background. It's a light, creamy color that feels clean and natural.

Typography

Headings: 'Merriweather', serif

I chose Merriweather for headings because it feels classic and established, but is still really easy to read.

Body Text: 'Lato', sans-serif

For all the main text, I'm using Lato. It's a clean, friendly font that's great for reading longer articles without straining your eyes.
